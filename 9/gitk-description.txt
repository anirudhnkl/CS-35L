The window is divided into halves horizontally. The top half is divided into 3 columns. The first has a branching tree structure of commits
and their relationship with the branches. The second column has the name and email of the author of the commit shown in the first column.
The third column shows the date of the commit show in the first column. 

The separating bar in the middle has the SHA1 ID fo the selected commit and the row number.

The bottom half is separated into two columns. The first shows the changelog of the commits and the diff of the commit for the modified files.
The second column has the names of the modified files in the commit.
